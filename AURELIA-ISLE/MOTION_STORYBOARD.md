# AURELIA motion storyboard

## Reference-derived, clean-room sequence

| Keyframe | Scroll | Media | Copy/UI | User meaning |
|---|---:|---|---|---|
| K0 | 0.00 | wide island | title + CTA | понять место и действие |
| K1 | 0.35 | crop moves toward shore | copy reduces | почувствовать прибытие |
| K2 | 0.65 | water/house detail | media dominates | увидеть объект |
| K3 | 0.90 | wide route context | coordinates return | понять путь |
| K4 | 1.00 | next section | CTA/next action | продолжить сценарий |

## Acceptance

- subject remains identifiable at every keyframe;
- no crop hides the CTA before K4;
- fast scroll never produces a blank frame;
- mobile uses a separate crop, not a scaled desktop;
- reduced-motion keeps the hero image and narrative order.
