## Strong text sources

### Copyhackers — AI prompts for conversion copywriting

URL: https://copyhackers.com/ai-prompts/

Why useful:

- отдельные prompt-разборы для landing pages, thank-you pages, SaaS homepage, headlines, VOC, JTBD, abandoned cart emails;
- полезно как библиотека типов задач, а не как набор copy-paste ответов.

Caster adaptation:

- делить copy generation на конкретные jobs: УТП, homepage, headline, thank-you page, lead magnet, cart recovery;
- каждый job получает source pack, output format и proof gate.

### Copyhackers — value proposition formula

URL: https://copyhackers.com/2022/07/value-proposition-formula/

Why useful:

- value proposition как single statement;
- rule of one: один offer, один reader;
- формула value-specific proposition;
- emphasis on customer research and specific facts.

Caster adaptation:

- УТП matrix: audience-specific, outcome-led, only statement, before/after, risk reduction;
- fact-strengthening pass before UI publish.

### Copyhackers — startup copy lessons

URL: https://copyhackers.com/2023/09/copywriting-for-startups/

Why useful:

- value proposition as promise;
- simplicity, uniqueness, benefits over features, test/iterate.

Caster adaptation:

- headline/lead must say who, what value, why choose us;
- no broad startup claims without proof.

### Regie.ai — prompt engineering for sales and marketing

URL: https://www.regie.ai/blog/prompt-engineering-101-for-sales-marketing

Why useful:

- prompt elements: context, instructions, format, tone, constraints, audience, goals;
- sales lifecycle coverage: prospecting, enablement, follow-up, account management;
- continuous improvement.

Caster adaptation:

- prompt template must include audience, goal, tone, constraints, output format and funnel stage;
- copy tasks are iterative, not one-shot.

### HubSpot — AI prompts for sales funnels

URL: https://offers.hubspot.com/ai-prompts-sales-funnel

Why useful:

- funnel stages: lead generation, outreach, follow-up, re-engagement, close;
- useful as a checklist of funnel assets.

Caster adaptation:

- funnel scenario map must cover attention, relevance, value, proof, friction, action, receipt and nurture.

### CXL — AI prompts and workflows for ICP targeting

URL: https://cxl.com/blog/ai-prompts-workflows-for-icp-targeting/

Why useful:

- weak ICP creates weak messaging;
- AI should sharpen thinking, not replace strategy;
- validate ICP against best customers and disqualified leads.

Caster adaptation:

- before copywriting, define audience and current alternative;
- score whether message fits best-fit users and excludes wrong-fit users.
