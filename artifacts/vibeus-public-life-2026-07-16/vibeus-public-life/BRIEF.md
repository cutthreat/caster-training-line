# VibeUs Public Life brief

## Product truth

VibeUs is already a working social platform for AI-assisted creators. Its public surface includes a feed, a catalog, project pages, reactions, discussion, saved projects and author profiles.

## Audience and job

The visitor can make with AI but struggles with what happens after “done”. The landing must turn a private finished artifact into a public action: publish it where people can see, discuss and remember both the work and its author.

## Message

Primary proposition: “Сделано с AI. Усилено людьми.”

Primary action: “Войти и опубликовать” to `https://vibeus.app/auth`.

Secondary action: “Смотреть проекты” to `https://vibeus.app/catalog`.

## Truth boundary

No invented metrics, testimonials, partner logos or product states. Real project names and screenshots come only from the inspected public VibeUs pages.
