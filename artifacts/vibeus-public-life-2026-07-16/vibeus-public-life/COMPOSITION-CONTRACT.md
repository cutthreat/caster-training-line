# Composition contract

1. Hero states the transformation and exposes both real routes above the fold.
2. The manifesto names the post-build problem without blaming tools or chats.
3. The lifecycle uses four chapters: create, publish, discuss, grow.
4. Real catalog and project evidence follows immediately after the lifecycle claim.
5. The people chapter changes the metaphor from artifact to network.
6. The final CTA asks for publication only after mechanism and evidence are visible.

## Responsive transformation
