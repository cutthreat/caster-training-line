# Design hypothesis

The page should not sell AI capability. The visitor already believes AI can make things. It should dramatize the missing second half: human continuation.

## Visual law

A solitary artifact becomes progressively more public. First it is a private build. Then it receives an address, reaction, discussion and finally author reputation.

## Identity deltas

1. Lifecycle narrative instead of a feature grid.
2. A proprietary green code wall that breaks toward the viewer instead of generic AI illustration.
3. One bounded horizontal sequence where spatial movement represents progress.
4. Real product screens form the evidence layer.
5. Digital green owns the creation layer while vermilion marks the human action layer.

Design variance 8/10. Motion intensity 6/10. Visual density 5/10.
